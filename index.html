Welcome to The Rangebow Festival!

Join us for a vibrant celebration of arts, music, and food nestled in the picturesque heart of the Rangebow region. Experience a kaleidoscope of local talent, gourmet delights, and captivating performances.

The Rangebow Festival is more than an event; it's a community coming together to share creativity, passion, and flavor. From soulful melodies to breathtaking art installations and delectable culinary creations, there's something for everyone.

Local Artists

Discover unique paintings, sculptures, and crafts from talented artists across the region.

Live Music

Enjoy diverse musical genres from folk to jazz, performed by local and touring musicians.

Gourmet Food Stalls

Savour a wide array of delicious food and refreshing beverages from our handpicked vendors.

Get ready for an unforgettable experience that blends culture, community, and the natural beauty of our home. Check back soon for program details and ticket sales!
